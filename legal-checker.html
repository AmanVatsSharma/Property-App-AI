📚 Legal Glossary

RERA
Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016. Mandatory registration for all projects above 500 sqm or 8 units.
Encumbrance Certificate
Proof that the property has no mortgage, loan, or legal dues attached. Critical before purchase.
Khata
Municipal record of property ownership used for tax purposes. Required for utility connections.
OC (Occupancy Certificate)
Certificate from civic authority confirming the building is safe for occupation as per approved plans.
Title Deed
Primary document proving ownership of the property. Must be verified for a clear chain of title going back 30+ years.
